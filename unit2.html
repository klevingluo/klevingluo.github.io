Facial Landmarking Developments

Unit 2 was a more traditional research paper, in which we presented an argument, and defended it with academic sources.

We were to use all of our available skills to produce a piece of writing that would fit best in the current discourse that was happening in the field of our choice. Although this assignment is similar in structure to a typical high school english assignment, I found it very different than what I had done then. The experience of arguing the themes of a novel are very little applicable to making an argument in the field. Conventions like general statements and comparisons to the human condition are forgone for a more direct approach based in evidence and figures.

I chose an ambitious topic that I had some limited familiarity with: facial landmarking, but I accomplished most of my goals with the piece below.
