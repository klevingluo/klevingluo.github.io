OpenCV Discourse Community

Unit 1 was focused on taking an outside perspective on a field that we were familiar with, and analyzing the conventions of it. I took an in depth look at the website for OpenCV, a library I have experience working with. Beyond the reintroduction to formal writing, my peer reviews helped to realize it’s easy to become accustomed to conventions of a discourse community require explanations for outsiders. It’s helpful to take a look back and understand what background knowledge helps you to understand a piece of writing.
